# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of the Employee Management System. We expect all members of our community to be respectful and constructive in their interactions.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Asking questions and seeking clarification rather than making assumptions
- Providing helpful and constructive feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics or choices
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Any form of violence, threats, or intimidation

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at:

**akash.dev@example.com**

All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly. The team is committed to maintaining a healthy and respectful community.

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, consequences may include:
- Warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from project participation
- Removal of contributions
- Escalation to appropriate authorities if necessary

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, discussions, etc.)
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Official project events and communications
